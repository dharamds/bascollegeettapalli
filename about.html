The rules & regulations for admission are as per norms of Gondwana University Gadchiroli and college.


  • Students should fill up admission form with his own handwriting.

  • Necessary documents should be attached to the application form in two copies at the time of admission as follows-

    • i) Certificate of SSC examination (two Xerox copies)
    ii) Certificate of HSC examination (two Xerox copies)
    iii) School leaving certificate ( original and two Xerox copies)
    iv) Caste certificate ( Xerox two copies) if applicable
    v) Passport size photograph ( two copies)

  • Fees Structure for admissions is as per the rules of University.

    • Note:-
    1. All rights regarding admission will reserve to college authority.

    2. Admission will be finalized after prior permission of Principal and conformation from University.

    3. Online admission process is provided by college itself.

    4. Students should have their own email ID for completion of admission process.

    5. All fees should be paid at time of admission.

    6. Admission fees will not be returned if student cancels his admission for any reason after conformation.

    7. Semester pattern and credit based marking system are applicable to the student who take admission in Gondwana University, Gadchiroli.







  • Election of University Representative will be held as per University norms are by Principals order.

  • Regular classes of B.A. and B.Sc. starts as per University directives.
